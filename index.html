Understanding NPS

The only metric
that matters: trust

After each trip, passengers rate: "How likely are you to recommend this driver?" on a scale of 0–10. We calculate: NPS = % Promoters (9–10) minus % Detractors (0–6). Drivers with NPS above +70 earn our Highly Trusted badge. Drivers above +80 with 500+ trips earn Elite status.
